I have worked with a bank Tele marketting data, using which I predicted whether a person is a potential customer for opening a long term deposit account in the bank. This was a classification problem in which various algorithms like SVM, perceptron, probabilistic Minimum Risk Bayes classifier and Deep Neural networks were used. Apart from this I have also worked with unlabelled data from a wholesale distributor in which I applied probabilistic clustering method like Gaussian Mixture model in order to segment the target customers on the basis of their spending habits so that the distributor can make optimal delivery schedule with maximal profits and minimal loss. Sentiment analysis for hotel reviews was also done by me using the Naive Bayes and perceptron algorithm. The sentiment hidden in the reviews for books on Amazon was done by me in order to predict the quality of a particular book.
